6-Week Programme Timeline

A structured journey from Blender basics to VR/AR-ready asset creation, with personalized support throughout.

1

Week 1: Blender Foundations

Session: Introduction to 3D & Blender Basics

  • Master the Blender interface and navigation
  • Learn viewport controls and object manipulation
  • Work with basic modelling tools and primitives
  • Introduction to modifiers
2

Week 2: Advanced Modelling

Session: Intermediate Modelling Techniques

  • Polygon modelling workflows
  • Understanding edge flow and topology
  • Advanced modifiers and applications
  • Boolean operations and non-destructive workflows
3

Week 3: Materials & Texturing

Session: Bringing Your Models to Life

  • Materials and shaders fundamentals
  • UV unwrapping techniques
  • Texture painting basics
  • PBR materials for realistic rendering
4

Week 4: Rigging & Animation

Session: Making Things Move

  • Armature basics and bone hierarchy
  • Weight painting and vertex groups
  • Simple character rigging
  • Object constraints and animation principles
5

Week 5: VR/AR Asset Development

Session: Building for Immersive Experiences

  • VR/AR asset requirements and constraints
  • Optimization techniques for real-time rendering
  • Export workflows (FBX, glTF formats)
  • Platform-specific considerations
6

Week 6: Support & Professional Development

Individual Consultations + Panel Discussion

  • 30-minute one-to-one sessions with each participant
  • Personalized feedback on projects
  • Career pathways discussion
  • Panel with practicing digital artists

Between Sessions

Participants are encouraged to practice techniques covered in each session and work on personal projects. All sessions are recorded for review, and supplementary learning resources are provided.

5 Group Training Sessions

Each session runs for 2.5-3 hours with a maximum of 12-15 participants, ensuring personalized attention and support.

Session Format

Live Instruction (60-90 minutes)

Instructor-led demonstrations with screen sharing, covering core concepts and techniques step-by-step.

Hands-On Practice (60-75 minutes)

Participants work on guided exercises while the instructor provides real-time support and troubleshooting.

Q&A & Discussion (15-30 minutes)

Open forum for questions, sharing work, and discussing challenges encountered during practice.

Schedule Details

Sessions are delivered online via video conferencing platform. The specific day and time will be confirmed based on participant and instructor availability, with preference for weekday evenings to accommodate working professionals.

Typical Schedule: Weekly sessions on the same day/time over 5 consecutive weeks, followed by individual consultations in week 6.

One-to-One Consultations

Personalized 30-minute sessions designed to provide individual support, feedback, and guidance tailored to your specific needs and goals.

What Happens in Your Consultation?

Portfolio Review & Feedback

Share your work from the course or personal projects. Receive constructive feedback on technique, composition, and optimization strategies.

Troubleshooting & Problem-Solving

Get help with specific technical challenges you've encountered. Work through complex problems with expert guidance.

Workflow Optimization

Learn how to improve your efficiency in Blender. Discover shortcuts, tools, and techniques specific to your workflow.

Career Pathways & Next Steps

Discuss career opportunities in digital arts, VR/AR industries, and recommended resources for continued learning.

How to Prepare

Before your consultation:

  • ✓ Prepare 2-3 specific questions or topics you'd like to discuss
  • ✓ Have your Blender projects open and ready to share
  • ✓ Note any technical challenges you've encountered
  • ✓ Think about your goals beyond this course

Consultations are scheduled during Week 6 of the programme. You'll receive a booking link to select a time slot that works best for your schedule. Sessions are conducted online via video call.
