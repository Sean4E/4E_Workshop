VR & AR with Blender

Professional Training Programme
A comprehensive training programme designed to equip visual and digital artists with foundational skills in Blender, focusing on creating optimized assets for Virtual Reality and Augmented Reality applications.
6
Weeks
5
Group Sessions
1:1
Consultations

Learning Outcomes

Navigate Blender interface and utilize core tools effectively
Create 3D models using industry-standard techniques
Apply textures and materials to 3D assets
Understand rigging fundamentals for animation
Optimize 3D assets for VR/AR and real-time performance
Export work in appropriate formats for VR/AR engines

Programme Structure

Phase 1: Fundamentals

  • Blender basics & navigation
  • 3D modelling techniques
  • Materials & texturing
  • Rigging fundamentals

Phase 2: VR/AR Specialization

  • Optimization techniques
  • Export workflows
  • Platform requirements
  • Best practices

Phase 3: Individual Consultations

  • 30-minute one-to-one sessions
  • Personalized project feedback
  • Technical troubleshooting
  • Career pathway guidance

Phase 4: Industry Panel

  • Panel with digital artists
  • Real-world case studies
  • Career insights & opportunities
  • Live Q&A session

Who Should Apply

Target Audience

  • Entry-level digital artists
  • Visual artists transitioning to digital
  • Basic computer literacy required
  • No prior 3D experience needed

Technical Requirements

  • Computer capable of running Blender
  • Blender installed (free software)
  • Computer mouse (essential for 3D)
  • Stable internet connection

Delivery Format

Mode: Online via video conferencing
Class Size: Maximum 12-15 participants
Session Length: 2.5-3 hours per session
Schedule: Weekly sessions over 6 consecutive weeks

Professional training delivered in partnership with CCIS and Culture Works
(Digital Arts Advisors for the Arts Council)
